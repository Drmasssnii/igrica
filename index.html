Let's talk more about the game

In this game you have to survive a lot of situations life puts you in. You can choose which character to be and with all of your friends you save the world.

Characters

There is a two characters from which you can choose. You can choose to be either Jasmina or Šejla. They both have their own special abilities which can help you a lot.

Jasmina

Jasmina is a very strong character. She can lift a lot of weight and she is very fast. She is also very smart and can solve problems quickly. It's better to picke her when there is some stuff to move around or kick some door open.

She is a great leader and can help you make the right decisions in tough situations. She uses knifes and katana sometimes.

You can change her clothes when it's needed in the game because there will be situations when you can't keep wearing same clothes.
